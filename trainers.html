Yoga & Flexibility

Jane Smith

Specialty: Yoga, Flexibility Training

Jane is a certified yoga instructor with a passion for flexibility and mindfulness training. She has been teaching for over 8 years and offers a wide range of yoga practices including Hatha, Vinyasa, and Ashtanga. Her sessions are designed to help individuals improve flexibility, balance, and mental clarity. Jane’s approach focuses on aligning mind and body through controlled movements and breathwork, perfect for those looking to improve their posture, relieve stress, or simply unwind after a long day. Her classes are suitable for all fitness levels.

Cardio & HIIT

Nutrition & Diet Planning

Lisa Green

Specialty: Dietitian, Nutrition Counseling

Lisa is a licensed dietitian and nutrition counselor with a focus on helping clients achieve their fitness goals through proper nutrition. Whether you are looking to lose weight, gain muscle, or simply maintain a healthy lifestyle, Lisa’s customized meal plans and nutritional advice can help you stay on track. Rehabilitation & Recovery

David Brown

Specialty: Physical Therapy, Injury Recovery

David is a certified physical therapist with a specialization in injury rehabilitation and recovery. He works with clients recovering from sports injuries, surgeries, and chronic pain to regain strength, mobility, and functionality. David uses a combination of therapeutic exercises, manual therapy, and corrective movements to aid recovery. His empathetic and patient-centered approach ensures that clients feel supported throughout their healing process, while his detailed knowledge of biomechanics helps prevent future injuries. David is passionate about helping people return to their normal activities pain-free.
